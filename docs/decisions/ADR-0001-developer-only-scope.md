# ADR-0001: Developer-Only Product Scope

## Status

Accepted

## Context

The original concept could expand toward DeFi intelligence, portfolio operations, and user-facing financial automation. That direction would increase product scope, operational risk, and compliance complexity.

## Decision

ChainMind will be designed as a developer-only platform for EVM builders and teams.

## Consequences

- Feature prioritization will favor deployment, debugging, simulation, and engineering operations.
- Consumer trading and portfolio features remain out of scope.
- The threat model can focus on developer and team workflows rather than retail finance behavior.
